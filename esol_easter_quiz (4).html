What is Easter?
Read this. Then answer a question.
Easter is an important holiday in Britain. 🌸

For Christians, Easter remembers when Jesus Christ died and then came back to life. This is called the Resurrection.

Easter is also a celebration of spring and new life. 🐣

We see symbols of new life: eggs, chicks, rabbits and flowers. 🐰🌷

British Easter Traditions
Read this. Then answer questions.
Easter in Britain is a 4-day holiday — from Good Friday to Easter Monday.
🍫
Chocolate Eggs
People give each other chocolate eggs. Children love them!
🐰
Easter Bunny
The Easter Bunny brings chocolate eggs and hides them for children.
🍞
Hot Cross Buns
A sweet bread roll with a cross on top. Eaten on Good Friday.
🔍
Egg Hunt
Children search for hidden chocolate eggs in the garden or park.
⛪
Church
Many people go to church on Easter Sunday for a special service.
🥩
Roast Lamb
Families eat roast lamb together for Easter Sunday lunch.
